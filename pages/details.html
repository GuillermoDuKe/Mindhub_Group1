Soy Rebelde Tour 2023

The Mexican band RBD announced its return to the stage with a tour of 26 cities this 2023. Today, they shared the list with the dates and places they will visit in their remeetting Soy Rebelde Tour 2023.

  • Category: Concert
  • Dates: November 3, 4 and 5, 2023
  • Place of the event: Estadio Atanasio Girardot
  • Capacity: 47050 people
  • Assistance or estimate: 47050 people
  • Price: From $99.000 to $1.700.000
